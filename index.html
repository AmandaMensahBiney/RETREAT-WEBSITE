Tradition on Every Plate

You’ll savour the bold and vibrant flavours of Ghanaian cuisine, a true celebration of culture and tradition, tailored to suit every palate, including vegetarian and vegan options.

A Taste of Ghana

Imagine the smoky aroma of jollof rice wafting to your table, its vibrant spices and tomato-rich base perfectly paired with tender grilled chicken, fish or savoury vegetables. You can almost feel the warmth of Ghana in every comforting, flavourful bite. Dip into shito, a spicy black pepper sauce with a smoky heat that tingles on your tongue, creating an irresistible fiery sensation.

Flavours That Tell a Story

Feel the stretch and softness of banku and kenkey, traditional corn-based dumplings, as you pair them with freshly grilled tilapia or rich, tangy tomato stews. Savour the earthy, comforting flavours of fufu, made from cassava and plantain, dipped into aromatic soups like light soup or the creamy, nutty goodness of groundnut soup.

Global Cuisine, Your Way

Don’t worry, there’s also a wide selection of continental dishes from pasta, pizza, salads, rice dishes and the chef can provide specific meals to suit your dietary needs. Just let us know in advance!
